# DSA 210 — The Soundtrack of My Life
### A Temporal and Psychological Analysis of Personal Listening Behavior
**Selin Nardal | Spring 2026**

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes my personal Spotify streaming history (Oct 2023 – Apr 2026) to uncover patterns in how, when, and why I listen to music. The analysis combines raw streaming data with Spotify's audio feature API to test hypotheses about emotional and energetic dimensions of music consumption.

## Research Questions
- **RQ1:** Do my listening patterns differ across times of day and weekdays vs weekends?
- **RQ2:** Does my listening behavior change during exam periods?
- **RQ3 (ML phase):** Can natural listening "moods" be identified via clustering?

## Hypotheses
- **H1:** Tracks played 23:00–02:00 have significantly lower valence than tracks played 09:00–17:00.
- **H2:** Weekend sessions have higher energy and danceability than weekday sessions.

## Data Sources
- **Primary:** Spotify Extended Streaming History (JSON, via "Download Your Data")
  - `Streaming_History_Audio_2023-2025_0.json` — 16,126 records
  - `Streaming_History_Audio_2025-2026_1.json` — 5,298 records
- **Enrichment:** Spotify Web API (audio features: valence, energy, danceability, tempo, etc.)
